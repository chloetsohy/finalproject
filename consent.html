Consent and Ethics

This raises a HUGE concern towards consent - permission from the user. To companies, the term ‘consent’ is vaguely just an illusion, making the so-called ‘terms and conditions’ purely a forced agreement for consumers to sign on. With this being just one ethical concern, it is evidently obvious that there are even worse practices behind the camera.

Another apparent violation of consent is the act of eavesdropping on conversations. This privacy erosion negatively strengthens the authoritarian power such organizations have over consumers, completely shattering the already non-visible trust into even smaller pieces.
