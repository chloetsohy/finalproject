Google

The leading search engine - Google, is a prominent example of using user data to track activities. By trading its ‘free’ search function, us consumers in return provide it with sufficient data that they manipulate to initiate personalized ads for us. In doing so, our private experience is practically turned into data monetisation, all doing so without our full acknowledgement. Hence while Google greatly pioneers the tech industry, it is not often reliable to supply it with your personal information, unless risking your privacy for their profits.
