Tiktok

Tiktok, a social media platform dominating recent years, has shown algorithmic manipulation that influences the way consumers interact and view content on it. With a record of tracking user communication and interaction, it pushes personalized content onto that individual’s feed, specifically through the ‘For you’ page. Instead of randomized short clips, it is engineered to be a stream of content that exploits the individual preferences, resulting in the possible addiction of doomscrolling through the addictive and customized videos. With the platform’s presence skyrocketing these past years, it leaves consumers vulnerable to its dopamine focused content, revealing the persistent lack of awareness gap.
